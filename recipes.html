Behind The Whisk

Erica Theivendra

Baking has always been the one thing that brings me joy no matter what stage of life I’m in. We all get so caught up in our goals and responsibilities that we forget the small joys found in a simple recipe. Recipes aren’t just instructions for a delicious sweet treat they’re memories passed down. I can still picture baking with my sisters in our kitchen, laughing, joking, and sometimes messing up the recipe (oops!). Those moments are part of why I love baking so much. I hope this little blog helps bring some of that same joy, warmth, and calm into your holiday season especially when you need a moment to breathe. With love,Erica
